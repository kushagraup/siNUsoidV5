CodeChef was born to help programmers make it big in the world of algorithms, computer programming, and programming contests. Spurred by the belief that programming is certainly the way forward, CodeChef realized an early start and an active community are a great way to help programmers on this journey. Another way is to introduce a healthy dose of competition. Turning a programming-based activity into a sport will certainly attract more budding talent towards this fast-emerging, challenging domain. These were the very reasons that led Directi, one of India’s most prestigious tech companies, to start CodeChef in 2009. The vision has always been to encourage problem-solving and competitive programming amongst the Indian student and professional community. By the fag end of June 2020, CodeChef’s custodianship changed from Directi to one of India’s most popular and credible online learning companies, Unacademy.

The Association for Computing Machinery - International Collegiate Programming Contest (ACM-ICPC), now ICPC, also had a role to play in the birth of CodeChef. It was an ardent desire that Indian teams fare well at this Olympics for Programming, leading to the Go For Gold initiative of CodeChef. The performance at these prestigious competitions is a reflection of the quality of Indian programmers at a global stage.

The CodeChef platform is designed to help programmers in this quest and serves as the world’s largest community of competitive programmers.
