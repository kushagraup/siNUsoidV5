  • Googler

Location: Hybrid

Prize:         First Position -- Rs. 1500/-        Second Position -- Rs. 750/-

Dates: 19th November 2021

In an age where the name of a search engine has been included as a verb in the Oxford Dictionary, Googler is an event that shall test your proficiency in the art of ‘googling’. In this open 'google' test, you will be given 5 questions that can be solved by googling certain keywords or images. For those of you who have an eye for detail and have mastered the art of googling, this event is the perfect opportunity to flaunt your skills. So intellectuals, go Google... and solve the maximum number of questions to win!!!

Rules & Regulations

  1. No unfair means will be entertained.
  2. Participants will be given questions and a period to solve them.
  3. This is an Open-Google test.
  4. Answers to the questions can be found by googling certain keywords from the question or searching the images on Google.
  5. The contestant that solves the maximum number of questions is declared the winner.
  6. In case of more than one contestant solving the same number of questions, the contestant who solves the questions in minimum time will have the advantage in the judging process.
  7. The time to solve all the questions will be conveyed on the day of the event only.
  8. The decision of the organizers in declaring the results will be final. No queries in this regard will be entertained.
