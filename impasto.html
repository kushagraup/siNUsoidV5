"Art is freedom. Being able to bend things most people see as a straight line."

Blending creativity and technology together, Impasto is an Online Digital Art Competition which allows artists to express themselves through digital and video art. Students are required to display their imagination through posters, logos or videos based on a particular theme made using digital art software. They say pictures speak a thousand words, come and prove it.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Students are required to make attractive posters and logos within 2 hours.
  2. The judges shall decide which poster or logo is the best.
  3. Students are allowed to use any poster making software with which they are comfortable working.
  4. Participants will be judged on design, authenticity, execution style and relevance to the theme.
