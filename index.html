Abstruse

Abstruse is an online cryptic hunt which poses as a mind-boggling challenge for any problem solver. It is a four day event full of cryptic puzzles and conundrums covering a wide spectrum of topics. Hosted on the main website of siNUsoid, the participants can use any tool to solve the problem. Based on a spectrum of topics : from prehistory to the future, from technology to pop culture and many more, cryptic hunt requires the participant to solve different levels using hints from different parts of the page. The one to solve maximum number of questions in the minimum time wins.

CTF

CTF stands for Capture The Flag, a style of hacking event where you have one goal: hack in and find the flag. Flags are placed in various locations -- they might be in a file, in the database, stuck into source code, or otherwise -- and your goal is to hunt them all down.

Iter Ignotus

We are living in a world where technology is slowly but steadily taking control of things at all levels, from molecular to astronomical. Its role has always been fixing things, making things better. But what if, this very control, turns feral? What if it changes our world into a different dimension? A different world with organisms we’ve never seen, phenomena science has never discovered and technology we never deemed possible?

Iter Ignotus, a journey to the world unknown, is what siNUsoid brings to you as its theme for this year. Because technology turned feral can only be fixed by technology itself! So all tech enthusiasts, come together as we travel to a world we never imagined, dodge extra-terrestrial attacks and solve the secrets of this new world with only one weapon - technology.

siNUsoid v5

Keeping the current pandemic in mind, NIIT University brings to you its annual technological fest - siNUsoid v5.0. Hosted digitally for the second time, with its theme as ‘Iter Ignotus’ this year, we strive to bring all of us closer together via a virtual connection. It is an initiative that aims to help students gain an understanding of the true essence of modern technology. siNUsoid is a platform for true technology enthusiasts to try, learn, and tread forward. All of our events have been curated to push the participants out of their comfort zone and help them reach the peak of their capabilities.
