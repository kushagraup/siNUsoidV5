siNusoid, NIIT University is a platform for true technology enthusiasts to try, learn, and tread forward but implementing this idea in the real world requires a lot of hard work and dedication. The Volunteer Program* is curated for you to connect with several people and improve your technical, marketing, publicity and social media skills. You’re exposed to leadership and management skills which will help you in your future endeavors. The Volunteer Program* helps you be a part of everything that goes behind organising a tech fest and helps you learn something new everyday. Register below to be a part of our team.

*Open only for NIIT University’s students.
