Biomaze is a biology-based quiz for all the Bio Nerds. The topics of the quiz revolve around preliminary biologies such as ecology and environment and general knowledge questions. So come test your bio knowledge and win amazing prizes while also learning more about the planet!!

This bio quiz will be divided into three rounds(TBD) :
1st Round – 20 questions, 20 mins with negative marking.
2nd Round – Hosted on Kahoot. Each question had 1000 points. The faster the individual answered the more points they got. There are 40 questions in this round.

Rules & Regulations

  1. The event is open to students of any academic year/ course.
  2. It is an individual event.
  3. Event organizers reserve the right to disqualify anyone that was found guilty of violating the rules/sharing answers.
